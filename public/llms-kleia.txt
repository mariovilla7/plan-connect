## Audience

Independent nutritionists, dietitians and licensed health professionals — qualified and legally authorized to prescribe nutritional guidelines in their jurisdiction. Kleia is a professional support tool, not a self-diagnosis tool for end consumers.

## Problem

Independent nutritionists spend hours on the mechanical part of building plans: calculating macros, reformatting documents, copy-pasting into WhatsApp, rebuilding shopping lists every time a dish changes. This eats into the time they should spend on clinical judgment and patient follow-up.

## Value proposition

- Clinical precision in seconds: kcal, macros and micros calculated automatically.
- Plans with local context: respects patient preferences and local food culture.
- Smart substitutions: edit a dish, macros recalculate in real time.
- Automatic shopping lists: every ingredient summed exactly.
- Effortless education: embedded patient guidance, no copy-paste.
- 1-click delivery: ready-to-share PDF sent via WhatsApp or email.
- Unlimited patient history and a smart recipe book.

## FAQ (canonical answers)

- Do I need to be tech-savvy? No — if you can use WhatsApp, you can use Kleia.
- What about my patients' data? It's yours, encrypted under clinical standards, never shared.
- Can I cancel anytime? Yes, during the pilot, with no penalty.
- Does Kleia replace professional judgment? No — Kleia automates the mechanical part; you decide what's best for each patient.
- When will it be available for everyone? Currently in a closed pilot with limited spots; contact via WhatsApp for early access.

## Disclaimer

Kleia is a professional support tool. AI-generated plans must always be reviewed and validated by a qualified nutrition professional before being delivered to a patient.
